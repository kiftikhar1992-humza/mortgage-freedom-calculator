About This Site

Mortgage Freedom Calculator is built and maintained by Adam Abrahim, a mortgage industry professional with over 20 years of experience. The site exists to give everyday homebuyers and homeowners the same financial clarity that industry insiders have — for free.

All calculator tools run entirely in your browser. No financial data you enter is ever transmitted to or stored on our servers. Rate data is sourced from the Federal Reserve's FRED database and Freddie Mac's Primary Mortgage Market Survey.

We typically respond to emails within 1–2 business days.
